About Community Photo Gallery

An official solution to fragmented photo storage — built as a centralized web platform for organizing, sharing, and celebrating community moments. This isn't a mockup; it's a fully functional photo management system where uploaded images are saved locally, persist after refresh, and can be deleted at any time.

Project Credits

Presented as a professional demonstration of a community-centered digital space.

Technology: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6), localStorage API. All images are stored as Base64 ensuring reliable offline-capable gallery.
